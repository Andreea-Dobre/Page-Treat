About us

To be honest, we are pretty new in this field. Selling books and encouraging new and ambitious authors to let us help them on their journey of sharing stories that they worked on for so long, putting so much effort in bringing them to life, is pretty terrifing.
It required a lot of patience and a lot of work, but we finally gathered all our will and made this website a reality, so you can now enjoy an online library.
You can sell your books here, or you can buy books, you can see our top picks every week and you can find out what other readers like yourself enjoy reading.
The choice is yours, the stories are infinite and the fun neverending.
